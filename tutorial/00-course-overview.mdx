# Course Overview: Prompt Engineering Mastery

Welcome to **Course 1: Prompt Engineering Mastery**. This course is designed to take you from a casual user of AI to an advanced practitioner who programmatically controls Large Language Models (LLMs).

---

## 🎯 Course Objective
Move past "simple chatting" into systematic, predictable, and programmatic control of LLMs. You will learn the mechanics, advanced architectures, guardrails, output controls, and orchestration frameworks needed to make AI work for you reliably at scale.

---

## 🗺️ Syllabus Outline
The course is structured over **4 Weeks** (2 lessons per week):

* **Module 1: The Core Mechanics (Week 1)**
  * [Lesson 1.1: Under the Hood of an LLM](./01-under-the-hood.mdx) - Tokens, Context Windows, Temperature, Top-P.
  * [Lesson 1.2: The Anatomy of a Perfect Prompt](./02-anatomy-perfect-prompt.mdx) - System instructions, Context, Tasks, Constraints.
* **Module 2: Advanced Prompt Architectures (Week 2)**
  * [Lesson 2.1: Few-Shot Prompting & In-Context Learning](./03-few-shot-prompting.mdx) - Programming by example.
  * [Lesson 2.2: Reasoned Prompting (Chain of Thought)](./04-reasoned-prompting.mdx) - Reducing logical errors using step-by-step reasoning.
* **Module 3: Dealing with Complexity & Output Control (Week 3)**
  * [Lesson 3.1: Delimiters, System Prompts, and Guardrails](./05-delimiters-guardrails.mdx) - Isolating context and protecting from jailbreaks.
  * [Lesson 3.2: Programmatic Outputs (JSON & Markdown)](./06-programmatic-outputs.mdx) - Exporting parsable databases and JSON objects.
* **Module 4: Real-World Scaling & Optimization (Week 4)**
  * [Lesson 4.1: Meta-Prompting & Prompt Chaining](./07-meta-chaining.mdx) - Linear pipelines and AI prompt generators.
  * [Lesson 4.2: Evaluation & Iteration](./08-evaluation-iteration.mdx) - Prompt matrices and edge-case validation.
* **🎓 Capstone Project**
  * [The Automated Enterprise Content Engine](./09-capstone-project.mdx) - Build a multi-stage prompt pipeline that transforms an audio transcript into blog posts, social hooks, and personalized newsletters.
