## 2. Methodology & Findings

### 2.1 Academic Citation Audit
The course does not present industry-standard prompt concepts as original discoveries. Instead, it accurately references the foundational literature:

1. **"Lost in the Middle" Effect (Lesson 1.1):**
   * *Citation:* Liu et al. (2023), *"Lost in the Middle: How Language Models Use Long Contexts"* (arXiv:2307.03172).
   * *Status:* Properly attributed and explained.
2. **Nucleus Sampling / Top-P (Lesson 1.1):**
   * *Citation:* Holtzman et al. (2020), *"The Curious Case of Neural Text Degeneration"*.
   * *Status:* Properly attributed and explained.
3. **Few-Shot Learning (Lesson 2.1):**
   * *Citation:* Brown et al. (2020), *"Language Models are Few-Shot Learners"* (arXiv:2005.14165) and Min et al. (2022), *"Rethinking the Role of Demonstrations"*.
   * *Status:* Properly attributed and explained.
4. **Zero-Shot & Prompted Chain-of-Thought (Lesson 2.2):**
   * *Citation:* Kojima et al. (2022), *"Large Language Models are Zero-Shot Reasoners"* (arXiv:2205.11916) and Wei et al. (2022), *"Chain-of-Thought Prompting Elicits Reasoning"*.
   * *Status:* Properly attributed and explained.
5. **Tree of Thoughts (Lesson 2.2):**
   * *Citation:* Yao et al. (2023), *"Tree of Thoughts: Deliberate Problem Solving"* (arXiv:2305.10601).
   * *Status:* Properly attributed and explained.
6. **Indirect Prompt Injection (Lesson 3.1):**
   * *Citation:* Perez & Ribeiro (2022) and Greshake et al. (2023) on indirect prompt injection.
   * *Status:* Properly attributed.
7. **LLM-as-Judge (Lesson 4.2):**
   * *Citation:* Zheng et al. (2023), *"Judging LLM-as-a-Judge with MT-Bench"*.
   * *Status:* Properly attributed.

### 2.2 Originality of Examples & Code
We audited key custom examples and coding patterns against common public repositories and learning platforms:
* **Meeting Transcript Example:** The standup transcript featuring *Sarah (DB migration)*, *John (Q3 launch assets)*, and *Lisa (API review block)* is an original creation.
* **Customer Support ticket classifications:** Custom samples representing Acme Corp customer profiles do not match any standard public dataset.
* **Coding Snippets:** All scripts (for OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google GenAI) are newly written boilerplate tailored to the specific variables used in the lesson text.
